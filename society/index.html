Education

The education in Baghdad is known to be very good. They have law schools, schools on Judaism, developments on college were made, schools for orphans, and even were open during invasions. They also had a multitude of other schools all for learning different career paths.

Women

The ʿAyyārūn helped the poor and women when they ransacked Baghdad, which is their most notable good thing that they’ve done. They describe that they treated them with “co-operation, patience and endurance”. While the ʿAyyārūn were in power, they also organized the higharchy so women would get their own "special ceremonies for initiation". [1]

Demographics

As established on the religion page, the population of about 1.5 million [2] which inculuded Jewish people, Christians and Islamic people. There were about 40,000 jewish people [3] living in Baghdad, with all the rest being Christians or Islamic.
